Our Story

Nestled in the heart of the city, Coffee House was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee has the power to bring people together. Since 2010, we have sourced our beans from small, sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting every batch in-house to bring out the richest, most honest flavors possible. Whether you are starting your morning, catching up with a friend, or simply looking for a quiet corner to think, our doors are always open.
